Instructions

In this experiment you are asked to choose among pairs of money machines. Your take-home money depends on your choices.

Each money machine has two possible outcomes, either €0|€32 or €0|€3.

There are two stages. In Stage I, please indicate the maximum proportion of bad outcomes (€0) you are willing to tolerate before switching from one money machine to the other.

In Stage II, you receive pre-drawn samples for each money machine. When you are ready to make a binding choice, please click on your preferred money machine. There are {{Constants.num_problemsets}} pairs of money machines in Stage II.

After your have made all your choices, we will randomly select one of your decision scenarios for payoff. This means you should consider each decision as if it were your only one. If Stage I is selected, we will randomly select a pair of money machines and then let you play the one you said you would want to play. If a Stage II decision is selected, we will let you play the money machine you selected. The outcome from your chosen money machine will be added to a show-up fee of €{{Constants.showupfee}}, which together make up the total payment you receive at the end of the experiment.

Good luck.
